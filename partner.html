Partnership Information

Your server must meet these requirements to become partnered. CEO exceptions may be made.

Member Requirement

You must have at least 50 members in your Discord server. If you have another form of social medium, staff will judge based on that.

Server Requirement

Servers based on the following content will not be accepted into the partnership program: These include, but are not limited to:
- NSFW imagery (pornography, hentai, CP, etc.)
- illegal activity (anything illegal in the United States or United Kingdom)
- threats (DDoS threats, death threats, etc.)
- Servers dedicated to spamming

Drama Requirement

We try to keep Netsky a nice place for everyone to hang out and chat. This includes drama and arguments. If your server is causing or involved with lots of drama, we will not partner until this drama is resolved.

User Information

You must be a well known member of our community to become a partner. We do not accept partnerships from members joined less than one month ago.

Server Information

As we promote creativity, we are fairly lenient with the appearance of your server. However, we do have a few requirements. Your server must have:
- an established logo and/or branding. (don't change the logo's design every day)
- an established name
- an established staff team

Exceptions

While there are many rules to becoming a partner, exceptions can be made. If requested, a member of management can join the server and review it. If we find the server breaks a minor policy, we may allow it. We cannot promise anything regarding exceptions.

Conclusion

Good luck, future partners!
